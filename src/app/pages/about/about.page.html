About Virtual Medicinal Garden

Virtual Medicinal Garden is designed to make traditional botanical knowledge accessible through modern web experiences and interactive 3D visualization.

Why Medicinal Plants Matter

Medicinal plants are foundational to many healthcare traditions. Understanding their properties supports biodiversity awareness, preventive wellness, and cultural preservation.

Project Vision

This platform combines visual learning and reliable plant profiles so students and enthusiasts can explore herbal knowledge in a digital-first way.

Technologies Used

  • Angular (standalone + lazy-loaded routes)
  • SCSS design system with responsive layout
  • Three.js with orbit controls and animated scene
  • TypeScript interfaces and reusable services
